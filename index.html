FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What support do you provide throughout the publication process?
+
We assist end-to-end — from manuscript writing and journal shortlisting to formatting, submission, communication with journals, revisions, and final publication. You'll receive milestone updates throughout.
Do you guarantee publication in Scopus or WoS journals?
+
While final acceptance rests with the journal, we maintain a 90–95% success rate in Scopus-indexed journals. If we fail to publish, you receive a full 100% refund — no questions asked.
When is the Article Processing Charge (APC) paid?
+
APCs are paid only after your paper is accepted. We notify you once acceptance is confirmed, and you pay the APC directly to the publisher. We do not take any commission on APCs.
Can I choose the journal myself?
+
Yes. We provide a curated shortlist tailored to your manuscript's scope. You always have the final say — we only proceed with your explicit confirmation of the target journal.
How long does the process take?
+
Manuscript preparation typically takes 1–3 weeks. Journal acceptance varies from 4–16 weeks. We keep you updated at every milestone so you're never left wondering.
